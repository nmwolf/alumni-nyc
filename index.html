Needs and Goals

    (1.3 million records 1849-1915)
  • City Directories as resources for attesting existence of addresses, occupations (and people)
  • Spatial Data as more than just maps!

Historical New York City Directories

Workflow
  1. Use optical character recognition software to turn digital images into text
    (approx. 14 hours!)
  2. Use a computer scripts to automate conjoining multiple-line entries into single ones
  3. Deploy an aritificial intelligence compute script ("machine learning") to label personal names, occupations, and addresses
  4. Match addresses to NYPL historical locations dataset
